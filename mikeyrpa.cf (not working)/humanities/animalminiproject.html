Habitat

These unique birds, parrots, live in areas such as woodlands, rainforests, palm forests, savannas, grasslands, desert edges, scrubland, etc.

Weight, Sizes, and color

Parrot weight can be between 63 grams and 3.5 pounds. Parrot sizes can rang from 2 1/4 to 40 inches. Both babies and adults are colorful and many different colors.

Diet

A parrot's diet usually contains nuts, flowers, fruit, buds, seeds, and insects.
